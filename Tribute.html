Alan M. Turing

Father of Computer Science

Notable achievements:

Turing Machine

  • A model of computation that simulates an algorithm
  • Basis of general purpose computers

Broke the Enigma code

  • Decrypted Morse-coded messages from Axis powers' Enigma machines
  • Crucial in Allied victory in World War II

Turing's proof

  • Proof that some decision problems in computing are undecidable
  • Led to the development of a "universal computing machine"

Turing test

  • Used to determine if a machine has an ability to exhibit intelligent behavior

"Mathematical reasoning may be regarded rather schematically as the exercise of a combination of two facilities, which we may call intuition and ingenuity." - Alan Turing
